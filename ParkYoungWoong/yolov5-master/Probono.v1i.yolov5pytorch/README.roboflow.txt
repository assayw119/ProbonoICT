
Probono - v1 2021-09-19 9:08pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on September 19, 2021 at 12:14 PM GMT

It includes 478 images.
Price-tag are annotated in YOLO v5 PyTorch format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 238x416 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
